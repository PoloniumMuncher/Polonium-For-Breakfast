And finally, a disclaimer

Polonium for Breakfast uses uses some names of real world nations, locations, and organisations, as well as historical, political, religious, and folk figures. These are not meant to be an accurate portrayal of their original counterparts. The names are merely used for the purposes of humour or familiarity.

Furthermore, opinions expressed in narration or by the characters do not represent my own, nor are they meant to accurately represent views opposed to my own. The narration is frequently in-character or expresses unusual views for comedic effect.
