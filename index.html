Okay, what even is this?

It's a repository of fiction set in the universe of Polonium for Breakfast, primarily stories and lore blurbs. Written with the betterment of humanity in mind, it hopes to answer the many burning questions that have plagued mankind for millennia:

What if the incoherent rants of a conspiracy nut or the ramblings of a stoner made sense? What if adding spikes and fire to things were inherently practical? Is chewing gum enough to hold a spaceship together? Where does the Sun go at night? What happens if you punch a god?

Ah, so it's a site for lolrandom humour!

Eeeeeh... Not quite. There are some internal rules, a level of consistency. Suddenly shouting "ha ha cheese!" isn't enough to drive a narrative or hold a setting together.

It's also not exactly supposed to be a comedy. While it's not a serious work by any stretch of the imagination, Polonium for Breakfast prioritises unusual ideas and the rule of cool over humour. The situations and concepts themselves may seem comical, but actual jokes only appear in moderate frequency.
